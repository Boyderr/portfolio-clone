About

Hi! My name is Emily, and I have been passionate about art and creativity ever since I was a child. I have always loved exploring new mediums, techniques and styles, and expressing myself through my artwork. This love for art eventually led me to discover my passion for technology and digital media. I have always been fascinated by how technology has transformed the way we create, share and interact with art and design.

I realized that my love for art and my passion for technology could be combined into a fulfilling career in media design. I have a strong desire to make the web a more beautiful and interactive place, and I believe that my skills in both traditional art and digital media can help me achieve this goal.

I am currently a student of BCIT's New Media Design and Web Development diploma program, where I am learning the latest techniques and technologies in web and graphic design, user experience and user interface design, and web development. I am eager to apply what I have learned to real-world projects and contribute to the ever-evolving web design industry.

I am actively seeking internships in the fields of UX/UI Design or Front-end Web Development, and I am eager to work with organizations that share my passion for creativity, technology and design. If you have an opportunity for me, I would love to hear from you!
